About Me

I am currently a postdoc in the School of Mathematical Science at Tel Aviv University. My advisor is Professor Zeév Rudnick. My interests are primarily in analytic number theory, the theory of automorphic forms and L-functions, and Quantum Chaos.

I was a graduate student (2012.09-2017.06) in the School of Mathematics at Shandong University and a visiting scholar (2015.08-2017.02) in the Department of Mathematics at Columbia University. My Ph.D. advisors are Professors Jianya Liu and Dorian Goldfeld.
